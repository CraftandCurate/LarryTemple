Larry Temple

Author & Artist

Welcome to the official page of Larry Temple, an award-winning author and artist.

Larry’s journey bridges the world of gripping thrillers and mesmerizing visual art. With accolades from the New York City Big Book Awards and Beverly Hills Book Awards, his writing has captivated many. Alongside his novels, Larry’s creative spirit thrives on canvas, metal, and more — reimagining masterpieces and crafting originals that speak volumes.

Explore his literary and artistic portfolio. Whether you’re here to browse his books, admire his recreations of Caravaggio and Monet, or discover his tech-inspired mixed media art, we’re glad you stopped by.
