Welcome to the official page of Larry Temple, an award-winning author and visual artist.

Larry’s creative journey blends the thrill of storytelling with the beauty of brushwork. His novels have received honors from the New York City Big Book Awards and the Beverly Hills Book Awards, earning him recognition for gripping narratives and unforgettable characters. Alongside his writing, Larry brings stories to life on canvas, using oils, spray paint, and even recycled materials to create both original works and thoughtful reinterpretations of classic art.

Here, you can explore his books, view his gallery of paintings and mixed media creations, and learn more about his exhibitions and inspirations.

Take your time and enjoy discovering the world of Larry Temple.
