Welcome!


Discover the captivating world of Larry Temple, where award-winning storytelling meets stunning visual art.


Larry’s creative journey blends the thrill of narrative with the beauty of brushwork. His novels have received honors from the New York City Big Book Awards and the Beverly Hills Book Awards, earning him recognition for gripping plots and unforgettable characters. Alongside his writing, Larry brings stories to life on canvas, using oils, spray paint, and even recycled materials to create both original pieces and thoughtful reinterpretations of classic works.


Here, you can explore his books, browse a curated gallery of his paintings and mixed media art, and eventually learn more about his exhibitions and creative inspirations.


If you’re interested in purchasing any of the listed artworks or have questions, see the Contact page.


Take your time and enjoy discovering the world of Larry Temple.
