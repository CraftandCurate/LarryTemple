Whom you may become is your willingness to try

Fail then fail once more

Silhouette of Joy (2023)

Silhouette of Joy

I wonder these myself

How do you define a life?

What is the measurement for success?

Know you are not alone in

Wondering what is and what may be

One thing for certain - never let your life become a

Cascade of tears hidden behind a silhouette of joy

Pick this moment to look back and realize life as it was

Need not be how it is meant to remain

End the cascade of tears and rip down the silhouette of joy

So, people may see who you have become

It's no veil of protection

Rather an impenetrable wall both real and imagined

Seek out connection -- know you are not alone

Learn early that regret is far worse than failure

Know you are not alone while frightfully working

All these years to keep hidden what must be revealed

One can wallow in peace

Two can connect -- feel -- share a cascade of tears and

Hold hands as you break down the veil

Raise your spirit and fly until you can

See past the horizon in all directions

Know the silhouette of joy exists no more and

Your path to true connection and joy begins now
